Frequently asked Questions(FAQ's)

What is Sachivalayam?
Sachiwalayam is a community platform designed to bring together people with shared interests and provide them with resources and support.
How can I join Sachivalayam?
You can join Sachiwalayam by signing up on our website. Simply click on the 'Sign Up' button and follow the instructions.
Is there a membership fee for Sachivalayam?
No, joining Sachiwalayam is completely free. We believe in providing access to our community and resources to everyone.
What is the purpose of Sachivalayam?
Sachivalayam provides grassroots-level services under various functionaries such as welfare and education, sanitation and environment, planning, and regulation.
What are the working hours of Sachivalayam?
The offices are open from 10:30 AM to 5:00 PM. Every Sunday and the second Saturday are holidays.
How many members work in Sachivalayam?
There are 1,26,649 secretariat staff working on a regular basis and 2,54,832 volunteers on an outsourcing basis, serving around 3.2 crore people, including home delivery of pensions and monthly provisions.
