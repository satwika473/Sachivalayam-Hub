Sachivalayam

Grama Sachivalayam (also known as Village Secretariats) are local government facilities set up in Andhra Pradesh to decentralize the administration by making services and welfare services of all government departments available at one place.

The Objectives Of Sachivalayam

Decentralization

To bring governance closer to the people by decentralizing administrative powers.

Citizen-Centric

To provide citizen-centric services and ensure transparency and accountability.

Inclusive Governance

To promote inclusive governance by involving local communities in decision-making.

Rural Development

To focus on rural development and improve the quality of life in villages.

Accessibility

To make government services accessible to all, especially in rural and remote areas.
